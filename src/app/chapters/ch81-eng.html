Chapter 81 : T2025—Other base metals; cermets; articles thereof

PDF (268 KB) [help with PDF files]

Supplementary Note.

1. In this Chapter the following expressions have the meanings hereby assigned to them:

  • (a) Not alloyed - Metallic substances containing by weight at least 99% of the respective base metal.
  • (b) Alloyed - Metallic substances in which the respective base metal predominates by weight over each of the other elements provided that the total content by weight of such other elements exceeds 1%.
